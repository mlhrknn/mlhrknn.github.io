Data Analyst

A data miner is a specialist who uses advanced algorithms and techniques to extract useful information from large datasets. They may work with complex data structures, such as time-series data, text data, or spatial data, to identify patterns, trends, and relationships that are not easily visible to the naked eye. Data Engineer

A data engineer is a professional who is responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the infrastructure that supports the collection, storage, and processing of data. They may work with a variety of technologies, such as databases, data warehouses, and data lakes, to ensure that data is collected, stored, and processed efficiently and effectively. They may also work with data scientists and analysts to ensure that the data is easily accessible and usable for analysis and decision making.

Data Scientist

A data scientist is a professional who is responsible for using advanced techniques, such as machine learning and statistical analysis, to extract insights from data. They may work with large and complex datasets, and use a variety of tools and techniques to uncover hidden patterns, trends, and relationships. They may also work with data engineers to ensure that the data infrastructure is capable of supporting their analysis, and present their findings to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner.

Data Specialist

A data specialist is a professional who is responsible for working with data to support the goals of a business or organization. They may have a variety of roles and responsibilities, depending on the specific needs of the organization. They may be responsible for collecting and organizing data, conducting analysis, and presenting findings to stakeholders. They may also be responsible for maintaining and updating the organization's data infrastructure, and ensuring that data is properly protected and secured.
